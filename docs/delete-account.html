MindPad Account and Data Deletion

Effective date: June 30, 2026

MindPad allows users to create an account and save personal notes. Users may request deletion of their account and associated app data by contacting the developer.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your MindPad account and associated data, email: wasilwa91@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your MindPad account so we can identify the correct account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When an account deletion request is processed, the following data may be deleted:

  • Your MindPad account information, including your email address used for login.
  • Your saved notes and note content stored by the app.

Partial Data Deletion

Users can delete individual notes from within the app without deleting their account. Users may also contact the developer to request deletion of specific app data.

Data Retention

Account and note deletion requests will be handled as soon as reasonably possible. Some limited information may be retained if required for security, legal, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons.

Contact

For account or data deletion requests, contact: wasilwa91@gmail.com
