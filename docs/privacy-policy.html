Privacy Policy for MindPad

Effective date: June 30, 2026

MindPad is a notes app that allows users to create, edit, save, and manage personal notes. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

MindPad may collect or process the following information:

  • Email address, used for account registration, login, email verification, and password reset.
  • User-created notes and note content, stored so users can access their notes in the app.
  • Basic app usage and diagnostic information through Firebase services, such as Firebase Analytics.

How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Provide account login and authentication.
  • Store and synchronize notes.
  • Improve app reliability and understand general app usage.
  • Send account-related emails, such as verification and password reset emails.

Third-Party Services

MindPad uses Firebase services provided by Google, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Analytics.

Firebase may collect information according to Google's privacy practices. You can learn more here: Firebase Privacy and Security.

Sharing

MindPad includes a share feature that allows users to share note text using the device's native sharing options. Notes are only shared when the user chooses to share them.

Data Retention and Deletion

Notes and account information may be stored as long as the user keeps an account in the app. Users may delete notes from within the app. For account or data deletion requests, contact the app developer.

Children's Privacy

MindPad is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use Firebase services to help protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
